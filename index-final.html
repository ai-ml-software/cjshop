Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to place an order?

No. Guest checkout is available for every order — add items to your cart and check out with your shipping and payment details. Creating an account afterward is optional.

What payment methods does VELORA accept?

Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX), secure payment links, and Cash on Delivery in selected countries.

Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available in my country?

Cash on Delivery is available in selected high-trust regions, including the UAE, Saudi Arabia, India, Mexico, Brazil, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Bahrain, Pakistan, and Nigeria.

What is VELORA's return policy?

Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery if unused and in original packaging. Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days.

How do I track my order?

Use the order tracking page with your order number and email address — no account is required to track a guest order.
